Submit a Report

Report suspected electoral manipulation. Your report will be forwarded to the relevant authorities. You may remain anonymous.

What to report

  • Vote buying or material incentives offered for votes
  • Organized voter transportation with payment
  • Pressure from employers or local officials on how to vote
  • Coordinated disinformation or fake news campaigns
  • Use of public resources for campaign activities
  • Suspicious campaign finance or undeclared funding
