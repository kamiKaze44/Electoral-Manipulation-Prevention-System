Submit a Report

Report suspected electoral manipulation. Your report will be analyzed by AI and forwarded to the relevant authorities. You may remain anonymous.

What to report

  • Vote buying or material incentives offered for votes
  • Organized voter transportation with payment
  • Pressure from employers or local officials on how to vote
  • Coordinated disinformation or fake news campaigns
  • Use of public resources for campaign activities
  • Suspicious campaign finance or undeclared funding
  • Illegal changes to electoral lists
  • Foreign interference or funding

Your privacy

You may leave your name blank to submit anonymously. Reports are stored securely and are only accessible through this system. Your IP address is not logged.

After submission

1 Report stored in secure database
2 Notification sent to monitoring authority
3 AI analysis available on request
4 Status tracked until resolution
Be as specific as possible. More detail = more effective AI analysis and authority response.
